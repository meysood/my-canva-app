1. Information We Collect

Frame Maker processes images (PNG files) that you upload to convert them into Canva-compatible frames. Images are processed in real-time and are not stored on our servers after processing is complete.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Uploaded images are used solely for converting them to vector frame shapes
  • No images are saved, stored, or shared with third parties
  • We do not collect personal information beyond what Canva provides

3. Data Storage

  • Frame library data is stored locally in your browser (localStorage)
  • No data is transmitted to external analytics services

4. Third-Party Services

Frame Maker operates within the Canva platform and adheres to Canva's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

All uploads are transmitted over HTTPS. Images are processed in memory and discarded immediately.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

7. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us through the Canva App Store listing.
